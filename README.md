of pulling text and playing it with TTS on an app. The most important dependencies <br />
are:

Major Dependencies: <br />
- Flutter 3.13.9 <br />
- Dart 3.1.5 <br />
- cloud_firestore 4.13.2 <br />
- just_audio: 0.9.15 <br />
- googleapis_auth: 1.4.1 <br />
